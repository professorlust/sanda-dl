What is Sandā Unleashed?

Back in 2014 I've released the free RPG game called Sanda. Now in 2018, I've ported the game from RPG Maker VX Ace to MV. This port provides better graphics, a higher game resolution, sideview battles, native cross-platform support, and some other quality of life improvements.

About Sandā

The rulers of the kingdom have mysterieusly vanished, and an evil might is uprising. Can you stop it and bring back Sandā to the state it used to be? (I know, the story sounds a bit cliche, but I've tried to give my own unique twist to it). The game also offers awesome metal BGM.

How and why is this game free?
I've developed this game in my free time purely for the sake of fun. I also don't think the game has enough content in order to charge money for it, so I offer it for free. However, if you want to support me you're free to donate (TF2) items through Steam.

Will there ever be a version for iOS be released?
No. Since I don't own a Mac to export the app to iOS, and neither want to pay for the fees of uploading it to the App Store. If you really want to play the game on iOS you can play the web version. However, please note that this versions lacks offline-mode and on-screen buttons.
